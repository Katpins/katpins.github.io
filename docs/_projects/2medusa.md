<h2 style="text-align: center; margin: 1em 0;"><strong>About</strong></h2>
MEDUSA is a highscore game in which you play as an abstract jellyfish. Points which increase the highscore are gained by collecting particles scattered throughout the game world with your tentacles through smart movement. Master the unique way of movement while the game environment becomes progressively more dangerous. 

<br />

**[MEDUSA placed 3rd at “Deutscher Multi Media Preis” in age category of 16-20.](https://www.mb21.de/wettbewerbsjahr_2018.html?articles=medusa)**

<br />

**My responsibilities:** Prototyping, Building player controlls, Building enemy behaviour, Implementing/designing UX, Sound Design.
