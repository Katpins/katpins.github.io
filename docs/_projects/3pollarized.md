<h2 style="text-align: center; margin: 1em 0;"><strong>About</strong></h2>
In POLARIZED player takes a role of a charming space hamster, shooting energy
beams at the space rocks, in order to charge them with magnetic
force, which makes the player figure fly towards or away from
them. The goal of the game is to navigate yourself through
4 levels and collect all polaroids. Different types pf space
objects have different effects, like black holes, which
allways attract and “eat“ the player on touch.

**My responsibilities:** Prototyping, Implementing/designing VFX, Level design, Building player controller, Visual development
