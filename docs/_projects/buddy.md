<h2 style="text-align: center; margin: 1em 0;"><strong>About</strong></h2>
BUDDY was created as verticle slice of an adventure game, in which the protagonist tries to get their lost friend back, by assembling their body parts into a new "buddy". The game lets you explore a bizzare fairytale environment, where you can talk to NPCs as well as solve quests to progress in the game. It is possible to solve quests in multipple ways, as well as to achieve several endings.

**My responsibilities:** Prototyping, Building interaction system, Building dialog system Quest design, NPC concept art, Animation
