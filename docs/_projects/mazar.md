<h2 style="text-align: center; margin: 1em 0;"><strong>About</strong></h2>
MazAR is an Augemented Reality based mobile/board cooperative game. It requiers atleast 2 people to play. 1st player uses mobile device to navigate themselves out of the maze, while 2nd player with help of physical map and a deck of cards defeats obstacles in the mobile level,by placing the cards on the specific places on the map.

**My responsibilities:** Prototyping, ARkit implementation, Engine implementation, Building player controller
